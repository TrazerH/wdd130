Overview

Purpose

Our purpose is to provide rafting enthusiasts with high quality rafting equipment.

Audience

We here at White Water Rafting are all "raft-heads" ourselves, so we know what makes certain types of gear important. We strive to get the best equipment to amatuers and pros alike, for a reasonable price.

What can I find on this website?

You can expect to find everything from paddles, to helmets, to entire rafts.

How do I know what equipmnet is right for me?

Usually certain items will have a description that will explain the recommended level of experience needed to use it. If that fails, we have a dedicated help link on this site for any questions you may have.

What happens if I can't find the item that I want?

Our stock changes monthly, so check back every once in a while to see new products.
